My Research So Far

AI-Powered Fraud Detection

Facial Age Estimation

Research Interests: Where I Want to Make a Mark

AI Safety

AI safety focuses on building reliable, ethical systems. Current research explores adversarial robustness and value alignment to prevent unintended outcomes. In healthcare, safe AI can improve diagnostics without errors, while in security, it strengthens cyber defenses, like advanced intrusion detection systems. I hope to someday create universal safety frameworks for autonomous vehicles and smart cities, ensuring trust and reliability across critical applications.

Large Language Models (LLMs)

LLMs are advancing communication with their ability to understand and generate human-like text. Advances in sparse architectures and bias mitigation are making them more efficient and fair. In education, LLMs can power personalized tutoring systems. In finance, they enable real-time fraud detection through text analysis. I’m eager to explore compact LLMs that work in remote areas, bringing education and healthcare to places with limited tech access.

Generative AI

Generative AI drives innovation by creating novel content. New techniques are making it possible to create everything from realistic images to complex simulations, all while keeping ethics in check. In healthcare, it generates synthetic data for rare disease research. In media, it supports creative industries with responsible content generation. I’m happy about its potential to model climate scenarios, helping farmers and cities plan for a sustainable future

Multimodal AI

Combining text, images, and sound in AI is like teaching it to think more like us. Recent work is blending these inputs to create smarter systems. In healthcare, this could mean AI that understands patient records, scans, and voice notes together. In security, it’s catching threats by analyzing video and text in real time. I want to push multimodal AI for disaster response, helping systems make sense of chaotic data to save lives.

Human-AI Interaction

Human-AI interaction aims to create intuitive, trustworthy systems. Researchers are working on making AI explain itself clearly to build trust. In education, it already supports adaptive learning platforms and in finance, it is enhancing user-friendly interfaces for banking. I’m curious about creating AI that supports mental health, offering empathetic interactions to those who need it most and I think it's already happening

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

NLP reshapes how we interact with data, from chatbots to analytics. New models are getting better at understanding context and even rare languages. Healthcare for instance uses NLP to extracts insights from medical records. It also powers fraud detection systems in finance. I will love to build NLP tools for local languages, preserving culture and making tech accessible to everyone.

Responsible AI

AI needs to be fair and transparent to truly serve people. Efforts to reduce bias and set ethical standards are gaining momentum. It helps prevents biased diagnostics in healthcare. I want to help shape global guidelines for responsible AI, ensuring it supports equitable systems in areas like farming and public policy.
